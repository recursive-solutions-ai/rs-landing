Hero Background Treatment

The Three.js orbs feel loud for quiet authority. What should the hero feel like instead?

A

Typography-Only Hero

No background effects at all. The type IS the design. Oversized DM Serif Display headline on a clean warm background with generous whitespace. Maximum restraint. Think: Stripe's homepage simplicity.

B

Subtle Gradient Mesh

Soft, slowly-shifting CSS gradient mesh in muted teal/warm tones. Barely-there atmospheric background that adds depth without competing with the type. No canvas, no WebGL — pure CSS. Think: Vercel's subtle gradients.

C

Refined Three.js (Simplified)

Keep the Three.js canvas but dramatically simplify — a single soft glowing orb or a slow-moving gradient field. The tech stays, the excess goes. Subtle mouse parallax. Think: Linear's ambient glow.

D

Fine-Line Geometric

Delicate SVG or canvas line art — a subtle grid, flowing curves, or abstract topology that slowly animates. Adds sophistication without weight. Think: architectural drawings, blueprint aesthetics.
