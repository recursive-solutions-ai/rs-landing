What tone should the v2 convey?

Recursive Solutions builds AI systems for growing businesses. Which personality fits best?

A

Quiet Authority

Think high-end architecture firm or design studio. Restrained palette, generous whitespace, typography does the heavy lifting. Feels expensive without trying. References: Linear, Stripe, Rauno Freiberg's site.

B

Editorial Elegance

Like a beautifully typeset magazine. Strong typographic hierarchy, dramatic type scale contrasts, refined serif/sans pairings. Content feels curated and considered. References: Monocle, Kinfolk, Apple Newsroom.

C

Warm Technical

Approachable but competent. Softer colors, rounded forms, friendly type — but with enough technical edge to signal real engineering depth. References: Vercel, Notion, Linear (warmer).

D

Dark Precision

Predominantly dark, with sharp accent colors and monospaced details. Signals deep technical sophistication and craft. References: Raycast, Warp, GitHub Copilot.
